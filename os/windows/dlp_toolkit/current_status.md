#### **5. Lifecycle & Teardown**
* **Status:** CRITICAL FAILURE (The "Zombie" Problem)
* **Issue:** The `DataSensor_Hook.dll` is remaining active in system processes (like `chrome.exe` and `pwsh.exe`) even after the Orchestrator is terminated.
* **Impact:** This causes persistent file locks and can destabilize the OS TCP stack.
* **Cause:** The teardown mechanism is failing because it relies on a Windows Event that cannot cross session or privilege boundaries. The hook remains "deaf" to the shutdown signal and continues its recursive interception loop.
